1 Slice timing info is off?

The "MULTIBAND_R-BOLD#1_EYESFIXED" acquisitions report a TR and slice timing that do not align. The TR could be 1.81 seconds instead of 525 ms. Or, more likely, we do not have accurate slice timing info. Despite the multiband sequence name, the each slices onset time is it's own. Mutliband should have multiple slices squired at the same time.

Also see a representative full dicom dump

4 Best guess TR

Were the slice times accurate, the TR would probably be 1.815 seconds: the later difference between slices + final slice time.

The average time between slices is less than the last. I'm not sure which is appropriate to use. average, last, first difference. The range is 1.7 ms 

library(dplyr)
d<-
  read.table(text=slicetimes,sep="\t", comment.char="") %>%
  `names<-`(c('protocol','time')) %>%
  arrange(protocol, time) %>% 
  group_by(protocol) %>% mutate(
    slice=1:n(),
    odd=slice%%2==1,
    df=c(NA,diff(time)))
